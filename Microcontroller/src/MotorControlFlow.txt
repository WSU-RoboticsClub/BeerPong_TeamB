This file describes the method for controlling the motors.

1. Pull Enables low
2. Set Direction 
3. PWM Enable to control the motor speed

Notes:
To coast pull Enable low

Control Diagram
--------------------------
EN | IN1 | IN2 |  Result |
0  |  *  |  *  |  Coast  |
1  |  0  |  1  | Forward |
1  |  1  |  0  | Reverse |
1  | 1/0 | 1/0 |  Brake  |
--------------------------
* = Don't care
